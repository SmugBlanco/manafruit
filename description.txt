This mod adds 3 things:

  ~ Mana Fruit Item
  ~ Mana Fruit Tile
  ~ Mana Fruit UI

Mana Fruit spawns at the same time as Life Fruit. They spawn in the jungle and can be detected by both metal detectors and spelunker potions. You can consume a maximum of 10 fruits for a total of 300 mana.
